About Us

Pawtrue is a youth-driven NGO that stands to protect the lives of five-sensed stray animals who are often neglected or killed. Our mission is to rescue, care for, and find truthful homes for these animals while providing them with love and support every weekend.

Adopt a Paw

We connect strays with kind-hearted, verified families. Each adoption goes through a truth-check process to ensure our furry friends find a forever home full of warmth and love.

Become a Parent

Weekend Care Services

Even if a stray isn’t adopted yet, we offer regular weekend care like grooming, feeding, checkups, and playtime. Volunteers are always welcome to help spread happiness!
